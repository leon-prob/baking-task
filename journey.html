All the ingredients

Greasing the pan

Sorted the egg yolk with the whites

Add a pinch of salt

Beat it with a mixer till fluffy

Poured 100 ml of oil in while mixing the egg yolk mixture

Added 2 tablespoons of mineral water and stirred gently

Mixed the flour, baking powder, and lemon zest together

Mixed it all together and poured the mixture into the greased pan. Put it in the oven at 180 degrees Celsius.

Take it out after 45 minutes
